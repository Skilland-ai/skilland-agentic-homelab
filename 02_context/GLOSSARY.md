# GLOSSARY

- Agentic Company OS: Long-term internal operating model where Skilland/Reboot can be run with resident AI employees across real business systems.
- Agentes residentes: AI agents with role, mission, memory, tools, autonomy, outputs, and traceable work, not one-off prompt functions.
- Codex: Resident technical operator working inside the repo and VPS to inspect, implement, test, document, and raise decisions.
- Divi-DClick: Downstream client/product vertical that may receive safe, sober learnings from HomeLab but does not govern the lab.
- Hermes: Central system HomeLab must research and understand deeply; expected to coordinate, route, interpret, and maintain agentic context, pending practical validation.
- Hermes Intimacy: Practical microhito for installing, configuring, breaking, observing, and recovering Hermes on the VPS.
- Hermes Literacy: Research microhito for understanding Hermes concepts, docs, patterns, examples, limits, and community practices before installation work.
- HomeLab: Internal, breakable Skilland/Reboot lab on the real VPS for turning North Star into agentic capabilities and practical learning.
- Kapso: Mentioned future WhatsApp/human channel path. Exact role remains unknown.
- Meta-harness: Future coordination layer for multi-repo work. It is a learning target, not an initial architecture.
- Microhito: Small milestone/run that creates concrete learning and leaves lightweight notes.
- North Star: Strategic compass repo/vision for Skilland Agentic OS; not the operational HomeLab or VPS architecture.
- Repos como fábricas: View of repositories as operational departments/factories containing context, decisions, specs, outputs, scripts, data, memory, campaigns, and deliverables.
- Sede agentic viva: A VPS/repo environment that can be understood and operated by human direction plus resident AI agents.
- YOLO interno: High-trust internal operating mode that allows Codex/agentes to read, write, test, and break recoverable things while preserving human approval for external or sensitive effects.
